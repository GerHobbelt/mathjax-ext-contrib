This sample loads MathJax version 2.0 from the CDN, and makes menu modifications specific to its contextual menu. Look for the item "get image" after the "Show Math As" submenu item. \[ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2}\, dx=\sqrt{\pi} \] Note: This sample uses an installation of John Forkosh's mathTeX on the server cs.jsu.edu to generate a GIF image of the LaTeX expression. When you select "get image", the image generated appears in a new browser tab.
